Our Initiatives

True to Sound Quality

We strive to distribute sound in its purest form without any compromise. It is our goal to foresee that the music produced in studio by your favorite music artists is represented in its studio quality without depreciation; the way it was meant to be heard.

Reliability

Our headphones are made to the highest standards of musical representation. This quality and attention to detail are what separate us from mass produced headphones. Each set goes through a series of checklists to ensure that they are of the utmost quality.

Affordability

Although other headphones of this caliber are marked to a price for people with fat wallets, we will always ensure that everyone can afford the listening quality that their preferred musicians meant their music to be heard with. We also contribute funds to lower income communities and donate headphones to schools as well.

With ethical business practices and the best sounds engineers in the world, we have been able to create a line of the worlds most powerful, yet affordable headphones In the world. At Eco Instrumental we believe in the performance of quality headphones at an affordable price. Our products are inspired by iconic musicians from the days of classic rock, a time when music was for music sake, and we strive to continue this mantra in our traditions.
